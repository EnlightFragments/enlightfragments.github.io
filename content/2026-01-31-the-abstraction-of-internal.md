Body is what separates the world into internal and external. We regard internal
as alive subject, external as a dead object.

Observing the world is the same as observing your body. It will transform, and
only unbiased consciense will be left, indifferent to outside or inside.

In abstraction you set yourself as important, and everything else outside is unimportant.
One sided representation to deminish yourself.
