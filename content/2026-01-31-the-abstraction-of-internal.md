---
title: The Abstraction of Internal
publishDate: 2026-01-31T00:00:00-00:00
---

Body is what separates the world into internal and external. We regard internal
as alive subject and external as a dead object.

Observing the world is the same as observing your body. It will transform, and soon
only unbiased consciousness will be left, indifferent to whether it's outside or inside.

When abstracting you set yourself as important, and everything else outside as unimportant,
creating a one-sided representation that diminishes yourself.
