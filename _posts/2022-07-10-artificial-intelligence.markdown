At the time of rising technology machines became gods in the eyes of ignorant.
It's easy to convince yourself that allmighty machines will do anything for us.
That's true, but we should speak their language. Formal language of routine,
converted to format of processor. This language is fundamentally different from
what people use in the sence that it is limited by what math can afford.
Learning, however, is not a routine. It does not conform to formal rules.
That's why machine will never be able to learn. Deterministic approximation
of learning is all we can get here and use it for good.
