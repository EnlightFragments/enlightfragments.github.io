Human's intentions are determined by one's own aspires to be paragon. Human is not even social animal,
but rather an idea, delirium. An idea which claims to be "outside looking in".
Drawing start and finish lines, we, as humans, sucked into life long competition. That's when that time begins,
when most of us will forget this is the last time they got chance to do something that comforms with their own will.
Most of us will choose to create our egos and stop here. The only thing left to do then is to inflate it as high as possible.

Having inflated ego makes it much harder to do what you want. It makes you think "What should I do to be better?"
instead of "What do I want?".

It helps a lot when you understand your own intentions. Coverted motives.
That's when you stop to think about yourself as an overgrown experimental rat.

Take a look at what you see every day and ask yourself on what it is all about?
Which intention it supposed to have? And which intentions do you have?
And the world doesn't seem so boring anymore. Seems like the biggest mystery.
