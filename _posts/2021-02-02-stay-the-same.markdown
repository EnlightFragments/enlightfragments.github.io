---
title:  "Stay the same"
date:   2021-02-02 16:00:00 +0300
---

We're all the same identity at birth, but not for long.
By now you are a completely different person, and the other one was hopelessly forgotten.
Unable to stay the same, but having treacherous road ahead, there is wish to memorise oneself
in order to not get lost. And get back when you feel like you are lost.
