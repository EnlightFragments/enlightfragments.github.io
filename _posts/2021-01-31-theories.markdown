Theories is what we feed on. When we say that *this* is *that* because of *this*,
then understanding takes place.
We need theory to answer the question *why?*.
And the question itself provokes for development of theory.
Chain of explanation can be long.
*A* is because of *B*, *B* is because of *C* and *D*.
*A* is a placeholder for any question. Chain of explanations can be either limiter or unlimited.
It can be limited right from the startif you wish, it's just that such system can be deemed wrong.
Some theories actually work, other not. It's also possible that none of theories work, or every theory works.
Theory is a model of representation of the world.

Let's admit that theory of everything would not leave question unanswered.

Doubt appear when theory gets complicated.

When got rid of meaning you get rid of headache.
