We are thinging in terms, but never notice it.

Terms of ownership: every thing has a connection to someone, who we call owner.
Owner has right for making decisions, on how to use object of ownership.

Terms of questions: question is a statement which implies that response should be given
in a form that clarifies subject of question.

Terms of problems: problem is and entity which prevents us from living desirable life.
Every problem implies that it has some kind of solution, which is sequence of actions
in order to make problem disappear.

...

Being ridiculously obvious, these terms keep getting out of notice.
But when declared explicitly, doubt appears, if base of how human thinks can be
something more than just adaptation to survive.

Lets contunue.

Terms of adaptation: adaptation is a process of keeping organism from being degraded
in changing conditions in order to sustain ability to show signs of life.
This implies that environment and individual are separate entities, and individual
is the one who adapts to changing environment.

...

These are not just definitions. These are entire mechanics of how people think.
Although it would still be interesting to make dictionary of definitions,
even more interesting is to declare explicitly these mechanics and question
their reasonability.
