Being a word of a negative flavour, ideology is still unavoidable.
It has calming purpose, because it allowes to tell where you are and who you are.
These questions can get answers only inside some system of ideas.
Answers to these questions form a worldview.

- being an individual in the society
- being a living orgamism in the theory of evolution
- being a worker on a factory
- being a citizen in a government
- being a reader in a library
- being a member of the family
- being a consumer in the economy
- being a student in the university
- being a passenger on the road
- being melancholic in the list of psychotypes
- being cristian in the world created by God
- being exploited in the capitalistic system

Rich or poor, beautiful or ugly, it's still calming to at least know who you are,
so that you won't live in the complicated world, where nothing particular can be said,
and every judjement can be applied to itself.

It feels soothing to be among the rest of alike, marking ideology as reality.
