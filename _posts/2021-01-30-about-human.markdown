Human's intentions are determined by one's own aspires to be paragon.
Human is an idea. An idea which claims to be "outside looking in".
We will drawing start and finish lines.
We will be sucked into life long competition.
An the time will begin, when we'll forget who we are, and existence of our will.
We will choose to create our egos and stop here.
Then we'll inflate it as high as we can.

Which makes it much harder to follow our will.
It makes us think "What should I do to be better?"
instead of "What do I want?".

It helps a lot when you understand your own intentions, coverted motives.
That's when you stop to think just about yourself.

Take a look at what you see every day and ask yourself on what it is all about?
Which intention it supposed to have? And does it conform with your intentions?
And the life doesn't seem so boring anymore. There's a lot you can do.
