---
title:  "Worldview"
date:   2023-12-30 11:00:00 +0300
---
Worldview is unavoidable.
It tells you who you are, where you are and what you have to do.
Creates all the necessary meanings in order to live.
Lack of worldview would lead to mental paralyzation.

It doesn't matter if you're rich or poor, beautiful or ugly.
It's still calming to at least know who you are and where you are,
so that you won't end up living in the incomprehensible world,
where nothing certain can be said for sure,
and every judgement gets shattered by being applied against itself.
